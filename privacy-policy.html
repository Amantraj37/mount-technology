    • Privacy Policy

    Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

    1. Information We Collect

    We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and business details when you fill out a form, contact us, or use our services.

    2. How We Use Your Information

    Your data helps us:

    • Provide and improve our services
    • Respond to your inquiries and requests
    • Send relevant marketing communications (you can opt out anytime)
    • Analyze trends to improve user experience

    3. Cookies and Tracking

    We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to personalize your experience and analyze website traffic. You can control cookie settings in your browser.

    4. Data Security

    We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

    5. Sharing Your Information

    We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data. We may share your information with trusted partners who help us operate our website and services, under strict confidentiality agreements.

    6. Your Rights

    You have the right to access, update, or delete your data. To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@mounttechnology.in.

    7. Changes to This Policy

    We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
